📰 Newsstand

One-tap activation for SCCLD publication access. Passes (NYT, WSJ) last ~72 hours and are tracked below; everything else just needs your library card.

Settings

Fastest setup: save your card number + PIN as a password for rpa.sccl.org in Safari (iCloud Keychain). The library gateway then autofills natively and activation becomes two taps — no copying needed.

Tip: after you click through a publication's “Access Now” flow once, copy the exact activation URL from your address bar and paste it above — future activations then skip the library landing page.

Access is provided by the Santa Clara County Library District to cardholders. This page stores everything locally in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere.
